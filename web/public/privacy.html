Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

DroneDAA ("we", "our", "the app") is operated by CAVU CONSULTING LLC. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Data We Collect

Account information (optional). If you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple user identifier and, at your discretion, your email address or an Apple relay email.

Purchase records. When you make an In-App Purchase, we record the product, transaction identifier, purchase date, and expiration date.

Device location (optional). The app may request your location to center the map on your position. Location data is used on-device only and is never sent to our servers or stored.

Usage analytics. We do not currently collect analytics, crash reports, or usage telemetry.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not track your location history.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers or serve ads.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We do not use cookies or web tracking on the app.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Account management: Sign-in, session tokens, linking purchases to your account.
  • Entitlement verification: Checking that your purchases are valid.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple Sign In: For authentication. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple In-App Purchase: For transactions. Payments are processed entirely by Apple.
  • Apple MapKit JS: For map display.
  • Apple WeatherKit: For weather data.

5. Data Storage and Security

Account data and purchase records are stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Vercel's infrastructure in the United States. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS).
