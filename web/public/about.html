DroneDAA

Live detect-and-avoid alerts, ADS-B traffic, Remote ID drone tracking, FAA airspace layers, and weather — all in one app for drone pilots.

🚨

Detect-and-Avoid Alerts

Configurable caution and warning zones with audible tones and haptic feedback when manned aircraft enter your airspace.

✈️

Live ADS-B Traffic

See real-time aircraft positions from your GDL-90 receiver (skyAlert, Stratux, Sentry) on a satellite map.

📡

Remote ID Scanning

Detect nearby drones broadcasting ASTM F3411-22a Remote ID via Bluetooth 5 — no extra hardware needed on iOS.

🗺️

FAA Airspace Layers

Display Class B/C/D/E airspace, TFRs, restricted areas, and obstructions directly on the map. Pro feature.

📥

Offline Maps

Download topo map tiles and FAA layers for field use — fly without internet when connected to your receiver's hotspot.

🌡️

Aviation Weather

Wind speed, gusts, temperature, visibility, cloud cover, and hourly forecasts powered by Apple WeatherKit.

Compatible Receivers

Works with any GDL-90 compatible ADS-B receiver including uAvionix skyAlert, Stratux, ForeFlight Sentry, and Dual XGPS 190. On iOS, the app receives data directly via WiFi — no relay app needed.
