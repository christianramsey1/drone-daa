DroneDAA Relay

ADS-B & Remote ID bridge for Mac and Windows

The relay runs as a system tray app on your computer, receiving GDL-90 ADS-B data from your skyAlert receiver and Remote ID signals via Bluetooth — then forwarding them to the DroneDAA web app or iOS app over your local network.

Download
macOS — Apple Silicon DroneDAA-Relay.dmg · Requires macOS 13+ ↓ 🪟 Windows DroneDAA-Relay-Setup.exe · Requires Windows 10+ ↓
Setup
1
Install and launch — the relay appears as a tray icon (green = receiving data, yellow = waiting, red = error).
2
Connect your skyAlert — join your computer to the skyAlert's WiFi network. The relay listens for GDL-90 on UDP port 4000 automatically.
3
Open DroneDAA — on iOS, the app receives data natively. On a browser on the same network, open http://localhost:4001.
